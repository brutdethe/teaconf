The Power of Choice

You can choose to experience the event in different ways: by staying at Val de l’ Hort with us for the duration of the event or by taking advantage of what Maulévrier has to offer in terms of accommodation, meals and other entertainment.

But in any case, you will be cared for and well fed. Each ticket includes copious breakfasts, delicious lunch buffets, and small snacks during breaks to avoid hypoglycemia. Without forgetting community evenings, to continue the discussions started during the day.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us by mail, on Twitter, or Slack.
